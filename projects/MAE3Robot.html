Project Objective

This project involved designing a custom fixture to perform high-cycle fatigue testing on medical device components. The challenge was ensuring minimal vibration interference while maintaining high precision alignment.

Engineering Contribution

I utilized SolidWorks for the mechanical design and performed FEA (Finite Element Analysis) to ensure the fixture could withstand 10 million cycles without structural failure.
